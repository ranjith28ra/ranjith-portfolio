Projects

AquaSense

A complete ESP32-based IoT system that monitors water levels using an ultrasonic sensor and checks environmental conditions using DHT11, MQ-135, DS18B20, and a TDS sensor. Data is sent to Blynk and Google Sheets with alerts for abnormal water levels.

Bus Indication for Blind People

An assistive device using RFID and voice output to help visually impaired individuals identify approaching buses. Enhances independence and accessibility.
